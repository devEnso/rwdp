Ishi was born in Tokyo in 1962. He dropped out of school at an early age and joined a local gang and, a few years later, the yakuza. Early in his career as a yakuza underling, Ishi discovered a severed finger wrapped in paper on his desk. Later, after discovering that it had belonged to his friend, he decided to leave the yakuza and cut ties with the criminal underworld.[1][2] Ishi was inspired to start DJing after seeing the film Wild Style in 1983. When I discovered the film Wild Style in 1983 I found what I really wanted to do, to express myself," he said in a 2015 interview.
